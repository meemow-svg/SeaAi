## 📖A. General Description

  `SeaAI provides a quick, centralised, beginner-friendly guide for cross-cultural learning across ASEAN countries.
   Our purpose is to bridge communication gaps and foster regional understanding.`


  **🎯SDG Alignment**:
  - `🎓SDG 4 (Quality Education)`: to *Enhance* cultural literacy
  - `⚖️SDG 10 (Reduced Inequalities)`: *Break* language and social *barriers*
  - `🏙️SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities & Communities)`: *Preserve and Share* regional heritage 


  **👥Target Users**:
      
      Students, business professionals, & tourists

## 📱C. How to Interact with Prototype📱

  Follow these steps to explore SeaAI's full capabilities :

  1. 🌐💻Launch: Press `Start Exploring` on the Homepage
  2. 👆Select: *Choose a country* from the ASEAN dashboard
  3. 🗺️Explore: *Use the topbar* to navigate cultural summaries by category (Food, Clothing, Customs etc.)
  4. 💬Engage: *Use the `AI Assistant`* for queries **or** use the *`Culture Scanner`* feature to upload or scan media for cultural detection     context
